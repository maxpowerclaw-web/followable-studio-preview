FAQ

The sensible questions.

Can this work in any industry?

Not literally any industry. It works for businesses with personality, social upside, and a discoverable niche community.

Why does creation cost more?

Because real creative development uses more founder time, judgment, and iteration than publishing or engagement alone.

Do clients need to be in every approval step?

No. The whole point is to reduce chaos. The approval loop gets tighter as the voice becomes more locked in.

Can someone start small and upgrade later?

Yes. Many clients should begin in Engage or Publish, prove the need, then move into Create if the account needs a stronger content engine.
